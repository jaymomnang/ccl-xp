Love People.

God is unlike us but became one of us because of his love. Following in his steps means that we love each other even in the face of our flaws. In Christ, we have become a family with a heartbeat to share our struggles and strides and show God’s love to one another

Small Groups

Small Groups Are Safe Spaces: We meet in small groups, a safe space where we talk about God’s word, pray and share our lives with one another. You can be part of a small group here.

Courses

Made for More: City courses are talks, teachings, and sermons curated for your growth. As you begin and continue your walk with God, we hope the knowledge, wisdom, ad experiences of others help to answer your questions, deepen your faith and bring you closer to Jesus

Premarital Counselling

Becoming One: Over the years, we have sought ways to walk alongside intending couples as they decide to become one. Through these real-life mentorship and counselling videos, we pray you find the guidance and wisdom needed for your new journey of marriage.
